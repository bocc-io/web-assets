About Joey

Founder and Director of Performative Competence

Joey Boccio is bocc.io's Founder, Director of Performative Competence, and proud recipient of the "Preventing Sexual Harassment in the Workplace (Managers)" certificate, courtesy of LinkedIn Learning. With over two decades of experience turning executive panic into enterprise-grade tooling nobody ends up using, he has mastered the art of navigating the modern tech stack — and the modern stakeholder attention span.

He builds teams that outlast the executive revolving door, shields his people from middle managers' vanity projects, and reins in cross-functional corporate lifers before they manage to derail everything. He's fluent in Databricks, dbt, and the exact architecture decision that prevents seven business units from having seven different versions of the truth.
