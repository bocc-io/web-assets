About Joey

Founder and Director of Performative Competence

Joey Boccio is bocc.io's Founder, Director of Performative Competence, and proud recipient of the "Preventing Sexual Harassment in the Workplace (Managers)" certificate, courtesy of LinkedIn Learning. With over two decades of experience turning executive panic into bloated tooling nobody will ever use, he has mastered the art of navigating the modern tech stack — and the modern stakeholder attention span.

He builds teams that outlast the executive revolving door, shields his people from middle managers' vanity projects, and reins in cross-functional corporate lifers before they manage to derail everything. He's fluent in SQL, Python, and the exact tone of Slack message that says, "This is a terrible idea," without actually saying it.

When he's not reworking KPIs for the fifth time to match someone's gut feeling, Joey enjoys forecasting things that already happened, visualizing things no one cares about, maintaining a grim ledger of every pointless meeting endured, and discarding Claude's bad suggestions.
